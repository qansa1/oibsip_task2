MS DHONI

The Cool Captain

  • Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian former professional cricketer.
  • He is a right handed wicket-keeper batsman.
  • Dhoni made his ODI debut on 23 December 2004, against Bangladesh.
  • He led the team to three ICC trophies including the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup and 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.
  • He led the team Chennai Super Kings(CSK) and won the IPL title four times.
  • He announced his retirement from Test format on 30 December 2014.
